Calendar

Subscribe to the club's meeting calendar to see every meeting — past and upcoming — in your own calendar app, each with the book, who picked it, and the host. It stays up to date automatically.

  • Subscribe in your calendar app One click in Apple Calendar, Outlook, or Fantastical.
  • /meetings.ics The raw feed URL — paste it into Google Calendar's “Add calendar → From URL”.

AI / LLM context

Want to ask an AI chatbot for a book recommendation based on our reading history? Paste one of these links into any conversation to give the model full context about what we've read.

  • llms.txt Compact index of every book, with a URL to a detailed text file for each one. Start here.
  • books.json Structured JSON, one row per book, for analytical or cross-book queries.
